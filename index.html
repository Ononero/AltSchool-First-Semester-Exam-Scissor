FAQs

  • How does URL shortening work?

    URL shortening works by taking a long URL and creating a shorter, condensed version that redirects to the original URL. When a user clicks on the shortened link, they are redirected to the intended destination.

  • Is it necessary to create an account to use the URL shortening service?

    You can try out the solution without an account. But to use manage, and track your URLs and QR codes, you have to be registered on the platform.

  • Are the shortened links permanent? Will they expire?

    Your generated likns DO NOT expire.

  • Are there any limitations on the number of URLs I can shorten?

    No! Even on the free plan, you have unlimited number of URLs to create.

  • Can I customize the shortened URLs to reflect my brand or content?

    Yes! However, you have to be in either the Professional or Teams plan. Branding is not availlable on the Free plan.

  • Can I track the performance of my shortened URLs?

    Yes. However, you have to be in the Professional or Teams plan.

  • How secure is the URL shortening service? Are the shortened links protected against spam or malicious activity?

    Scissor is a very secured platform. Our servers are up-to-date with the top level cyber protection.

  • What is a QR code and what can it do?

    A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information, such as URLs, text, contact details, or other data. It can be scanned using a QR code reader or a smartphone camera equipped with a QR code scanning app. When scanned, the QR code can perform actions such as opening a webpage, displaying text, initiating a phone call, or sending an email.

  • Is there an API available for integrating the URL shortening service into my own applications or websites?

    Yes. However, this is only availlable on the Teams plan.
